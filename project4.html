Energy Visualisation

Visualisation energy usage feedback in a household and changing people's uasage behaviour

Overview

An investigation if providing energy visualisations can help people reduce household energy usage. Three types of visualisation designs were identified for energy usage which was Dashboard, Ambience and Eco-Visualisations. Prototypes were developed based on each type that could help residents understand their energy usage better and help make changes for their behaviour. A qualitative study found that residents are willing to have energy visualisations, but they all have different reasons for using it. Hence it is important that residents are given a choice on what type of information they want to see in their visualisations.

Research questions

  • How can future energy consumption data be represented?
  • Are these designs something consumers want in their household?
  • Could these visualisations try to promote behavioural change?
