Recycling App

Concept app of that allows users to get rewarded for recycling their items

Design choices

  • All pages includes a back button, to help users navigate to their previous action
  • Each page has a top title, to help users' know which page they are on
  • Users can checkout their credit from any page at the top, as this is the primary fucntion of the app
  • Colour scheme and icons are consistent throughout the app
  • To help show which buttons are not clicked, the opacity has been decreased
  • Shadows are added to cards to help improve aesthetics and highlight the important parts on the page

Cricket Information Page

These designs show an individual's profile and their performace analysis visually

Design choices

  • A modern look for player profile page compared to current popular sites such as ESPN Cricinfo
  • Integration of the player's social media account in bottom right, to provide a personal touch
  • Easily identify player stats in all 3 formats of cricket
  • Visual analysis page help show their statistics in an alternative way rather than just numbers - this allows to spot patterns and new insights about the player

Product page

These designs show a product category page and an individual product page. These are based from John Lewis mobile app with added design improvements

Design choices

  • Top half of the product category page quickly allows to filter through which type of product they want
  • The product page provides main details and gives priority to the images of the product
  • Users can quickly click change colour of the product
  • Users can click 'read all reviiews' without neediing to scroll down to the review section
